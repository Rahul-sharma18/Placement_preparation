Deepthi Narkuti

A Hyderabad resident named Deepthi Narkuti has bagged her dream job at Microsoft with a generous salary of Rs 2 crore per annum. She is going to take up the role of a software development engineer grade-2 group at the multinational tech giant. She will be working at the company's headquarters in Seattle, USA.
According to The Hans India, Deepthi had been receiving plenty of offers from AAA-rated companies that wanted to hire her even before she had completed her postgraduate in MS (Computers) from the University of Florida. These companies also included Amazon, Goldman and Sachs. However, after careful consideration, Deepthi chose to go ahead with Microsoft to launch her career as a software developer. She joined the company on May 17. Another fun fact is that Deepthi has received the highest annual salary package among the 300 students who got placed during the campus interview at The University of Florida.
Also, this is not the first time that Deepthi has had a taste of success. After she had completed her BTech degree from Osmania College of Engineering, Hyderabad, Deepthi had joined JP Morgan which is an American investment bank and financial services company. She worked there for three years and since she wanted to pursue higher education, she bagged a scholarship at the University of Florida where she went to study her MS program. Deepthi firmly believes that technology can have a significant impact in transforming lives, as mentioned in her LinkedIn bio.
